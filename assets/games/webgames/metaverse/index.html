What is the metaverse?

The metaverse is an immersive 3D real-time virtual environment where you can have an identity, a place and posessions. You can meet other people, play and build with them.

Shareability

One of the key aspects of the metaverse is the fact that all digital assets are interchangeable no matter the platform. People have the right to own, share or trade their possessions.

The metaverse aims to free the corporated owning of your data, making you dependant of the platform.
